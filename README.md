Brand Name: Eyla Creations
Founded by: Anandu Ajikumar
Brand Type: Independent Creative Design Studio
Focus Area: Brand Identity & Visual Design

About the Brand

Eyla Creations is a creative design studio focused on crafting visually compelling and emotionally engaging brand experiences. The brand explores the intersection of design, storytelling, and modern aesthetics, with a strong emphasis on clean visuals, thoughtful typography, and premium presentation.

What Eyla Creations Does:

Packaging design concepts
Brand identity & visual exploration
Product mockups and creative redesigns
Concept-based designs for learning, innovation, and portfolio development

Design Philosophy:

Eyla Creations believes that good design is not just how it looks, but how it feels. Every concept is created with attention to:
Brand emotion and consumer perception
Simplicity with purpose
Modern aesthetics aligned with real-world branding standards

Purpose & Vision:

The purpose of Eyla Creations is to:
Continuously learn and experiment with brand-led design
Build a strong creative portfolio through concept-driven work
Collaborate, learn, and gain insights from industry professionals and established brands
